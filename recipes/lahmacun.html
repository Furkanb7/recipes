Lahmacun

Lahmacun is a thin Turkish flatbread topped with spiced minced meat, onions, tomatoes, and herbs. It's baked and served with fresh vegetables, rolled up, and enjoyed as a tasty meal.

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat (beef or lamb)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs (such as parsley and mint)
  • Spices
  • Thin flatbread (like pita or lavash)

Steps on the Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Meat Topping:

    Combine minced meat (beef or lamb) with finely chopped onions, tomatoes, herbs (such as parsley and mint), and spices. Mix the ingredients well to create a flavorful topping.

  2. Prepare the Dough:

    Roll out thin flatbread (like pita or lavash) to create a base for the lahmacun.

  3. Spread the Meat Topping:

    Evenly spread the prepared meat topping over the rolled-out flatbread, covering the entire surface.

  4. Bake the Lahmacun:

    Bake the lahmacun in a preheated oven until the edges of the flatbread are crispy and golden brown, and the topping is cooked through.

  5. Serve and Enjoy:

    Once baked, remove the lahmacun from the oven and let it cool briefly. Slice it into wedges and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious homemade lahmacun!
